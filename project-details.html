Energizzo – Reinventing the Energy Business

A smart software solution designed to digitize, automate, and empower how energy companies operate. Built from scratch. With vision.

The Challenge

Bringing energy into the future. In the Italian energy sector, many companies are still limited by outdated tools, manual workflows, and inefficiency. The challenge was to build a modern, scalable platform capable of:

  1. Automating all operational processes, from customer management to invoicing.
  2. Providing smart AI-powered customer support.
  3. Integrating analytics and marketing tools into a unified system.

The Solution

A tech startup built for energy. I designed and developed Energizzo entirely—handling branding, UX/UI, infrastructure, AI integration, and business logic. The result?

  1. A modular cloud-based platform with native AI.
  2. Intuitive dashboards and tools for sales and operations teams.
  3. Automated voice support and APIs ready for future integrations.

Project Result

A new standard in digital energy. Energizzo is more than just software—it’s a complete ecosystem. Today:

  1. The platform is already live and serving real clients.
  2. Operational costs are reduced by up to 40% compared to traditional systems.
  3. The system is designed to scale—every module grows with the business.
